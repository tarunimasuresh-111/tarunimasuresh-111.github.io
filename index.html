About

I studied Instrumentation and Control Engineering (B.E.) and am now completing my master's at THM Friedberg, Germany, specialising in automation, embedded systems, and communication networks.

My practical focus is PLC programming — ladder logic, timers, counters, Set/Reset logic, and HMI visualisation in CODESYS. I also work with Node-RED and MQTT for data pipelines and built a Python-based robotic arm simulation with real-time sensor processing during my internship in India.

Before Germany, I ran field projects with an NGO doing rural education outreach across Tamil Nadu. That side of my background — communicating technical concepts clearly to non-technical audiences — carries directly into how I approach engineering work.
